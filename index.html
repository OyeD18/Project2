W.Y.A.W.Y.W.G?

Where are you at, and where do you want to go? A digital portfolio all about who you are.

Role

Sole Designer – I made all the graphics and digital assets by myself.

Scope

2 Weeks, January 6, 2025 to January 20, 2025.

Tools

Adobe Illustrate, Adobe Express, Adobe Photoshop, Autodesk Sketbook

Background

This was a design-focused project that challenged me to define and refine my unique design identity. It has an emphasizes on personal taste, creative exploration, and the integration of Generative AI (GenAI) as a design tool.

Design Requirement

We need to create a series of 3–5 vertically-oriented 11” x 17” graphic designs that tell the story of your design identity. Each piece should be carefully curated and interconnected, avoiding disjointed or random compositions.

Core Elements that can be Included:

  • Self-Portrait – Represent yourself visually (photo, illustration, or graphic).
  • Moodboard – Showcase your taste: favorite designers, colors, aesthetics, and inspirations.
  • Design Philosophy – Define your views on design and taste.
  • "Good Design Is..." – Outline key principles of effective design.
  • Gifts & Strengths – Visually illustrate your unique skills as a designer.
  • Design Goals – Short-term & long-term aspirations.
  • Creative Origin Story – Share a compelling narrative of your design journey.
  • Design Principles – Be specific about which design disciplines excite you.
  • Themes of Interest – Identify key topics/themes you’d like to explore.
  • Personal Style – Define and illustrate your aesthetic.
  • Influences – Highlight and analyze your favorite designers.
